Year-on-Year Comparison & Strategy

The data tells a clear story of adaptation and opportunity. Here are the key strategic takeaways.

The Story of Two Audiences

Industry Guests are trendsetters. Their 2024 shift to Special Programmes was followed by Movie Enthusiasts in 2025, who validated the move by paying a premium for these experiences.

From High Volume to High Value

The festival successfully pivoted from a broad-appeal model (2023) to a curated, high-value model (2025). While attendance has declined, the financial outcome has stabilized.

Agile Infrastructure

Changing nearly half the primary venues between 2024 and 2025 without major disruption proves the festival's operational flexibility. DokuKino serves as a model for future growth.

Final Strategic Recommendations

1. Trust Your Industry Guests as a Leading Indicator

Use complimentary tickets strategically. Their choices are your best free market research for what will be valued next year. Analyze their behavior for insights.

2. Market the Value, Not Just the Films

Your audience now pays for a premium, curated experience. Emphasize the uniqueness of special programmes and the quality of curation to justify ticket prices.

3. Stabilize the Enthusiast Audience

The most pressing challenge is halting the decline in paid ticket sales. Target marketing towards enthusiasts who love unique cinematic experiences.
